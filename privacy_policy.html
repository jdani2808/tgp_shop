This policy explains what information we collect when you use TGP online store site. It also has information about how we store, use, transfer, and delete that information. Our aim is not just to comply privacy law. It's to earn your trust.

Information We Collect & We Use It

In order to you the best possible experience using TGP online store, we collect information from your interactions with our network. Some of this information, you actively tell us (such as your email address which we use to track your account or communicate with you). Other information, we collect based on actions you take while using TGP online store, such as what pages you access and your interactions with our product. This information includes records of those interactions, your Internet Protocol address, information about your device.
